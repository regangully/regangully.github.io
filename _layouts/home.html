About Me

Hello! My name is Regan, funnily enough. I am a kiwi Software Engineer who currently lives and works in little-ole' Aotearoa, New Zealand.

I have always had a passion for creating things. Nowadays I use this creativity to design and develop software to impact, excite and positively influence people.

When I'm not busy developing I like to pass-the-time having a hit around at tennis, and cheering on the greatest-of-all-time, Roger Federer. I also like to embrace my inner rock-star and strum out a tune on my guitar when I get the chance.
